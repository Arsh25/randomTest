Intro for Students

The Project

This project is designed to give you a unique experience of developing robots to interact with humans in their daily lives.

Most robots work in places like factories where they interact little with humans. We want to build robots that are helpful to humans on a much closer level.

The Robots

In this project you will develop two robots: Quizbot, an introductory robot designed to help students in studying for a test, and KT, a telepresence robot designed to let people take a journey in a remote place.
